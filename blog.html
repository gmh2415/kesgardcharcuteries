My Favorite Products to Use on a Board February 13th, 2022

The fun thing about creating and decorating charcuterie boards are the endless products you can use on the boards. Whether it is a fun dip, jam, or spread or a new cracker you found, there are endless possibilites to what a board can look like. Below I share what my favorite products to use are.

1. Divina Fig Spread

This spread is full of flavor! Although figs tend to turn some people off Divina's fig spread is rich in carmel flavors which pairs wonderfully with cheese and wine!

2. Texas Pepper Works Candy Crisp Jalapenos

These Jalapenos bring sweet and spicy together in the perfect mix! Not only do I love using these on my charcuterie boards, but I love them as a snack as well with some cream cheese on a cracker.

3. Trader Joes Roasted & Salted Truffle Marcona Almonds

I LOVE truffle. Whether its truffle oil fries, pasta, or in this case nuts! These are perfect for snacking on and bring an interesting flavor to the board.

4. Robert Rothschild Farm Raspberry Honey Mustard Dip and Glaze

There is this little shop in the town of Leavenworth, WA that lets you test more than 20 different mustards, honeys, and jams. Using a mustard on my board brings that nostalgia back, a comfort caused by both food and memory.

5. La Panzanella Rosemary Crackers

These crackers are something I've used for years. My mom use to use these on her charcuterie boards and I have carried on the tradition. They are so simple, yet so flavorful and are paired well with any cheese!
